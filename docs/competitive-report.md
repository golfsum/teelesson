Date: 2026-07-13  
Market: United States  
User and buyer: independent golf coach or small academy owner  
Job: manage students, sessions, follow-up, progress, and revenue without moving
between unrelated tools

## Job comparison

| Alternative | First value | Strength to preserve | Gap TeeLesson can address |
|---|---|---|---|
| CoachNow | Create a student space and share media | Mature communication and video depth | Higher suite complexity for an independent coach who mainly needs daily operations |
| Skillest | Publish a coach profile or start remote coaching | Discovery, remote lessons, subscriptions | Daily in-person roster and practice operations are not the only focus |
| Onform | Record and analyze video | Strong capture, analysis, and team media | Scheduling and simple coaching-business context are secondary |
| SwingPath | Publish availability and booking link | Golf-specific scheduling and reminders | Less emphasis on longitudinal student progress and practice accountability |
| Generic booking tools | Publish bookable services | Reliable booking and payment patterns | Weak golf-specific student history, goals, drills, and coaching context |
| Text, calendar, spreadsheet | Start immediately with familiar tools | No migration or subscription | Repeated data transfer, missed context, weak accountability, and no shared student record |

## Product decisions

- Preserve fast scheduling, public booking, roster search, status clarity, and
  mobile access because these are table stakes across direct and indirect tools.
- Differentiate through a single Today view that joins the next lesson, practice
  follow-up, student progress, reminders, and simple revenue status.
- Keep video and drills connected to the student record without positioning the
  MVP as a full biomechanical analysis suite.
- Keep payment claims honest until a processor is connected. Paid status is
  tracking, not transaction processing.
- Omit academy-scale staff administration and launch-monitor integrations until
  validated demand justifies their complexity.

## Differentiation thesis

For independent golf coaches who need to run each coaching day and keep student
progress moving, unlike a generic booking tool or a video-first coaching app,
TeeLesson connects the next lesson, the student record, practice follow-up, and
revenue status in one focused Today workflow.
